About The Purrfect Kitty

The handsome devil in the tuxedo

He stares so intensely with his big green eyes that he sometimes looks like an owl. Especially if you are eating something - no matter what it is.

Grogu once tried the leftovers of some mulled wine. His cat parents foolishly assumed the slices of mandarin soaking in spices and wine would be safe from their fluffy monster. In true Grogu fashion he sampled a slice of mandarin as they slept before remembering that he hates fruit and leaving it sitting on the bench.
